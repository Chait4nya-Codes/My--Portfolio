Background

About me.

Hey, I'm Chaitanya — a CSE (AI/ML) student from India with a passion for Artificial Intelligence, Software Engineering, Startups, and building solutions for real-world problems.

Surprisingly enough, I did not begin with a passion for programming and technology. Initially, coding seemed complex and not worth investing much effort in. Over time, I grew fascinated with how software functions, the power of artificial intelligence, and how products that change millions of lives are made.

As my curiosity turned into genuine interest, I am now learning Python, Machine Learning, Software Engineering, and Product Development — sharing it all publicly as I go.
